I am a technology professional and author with a strong foundation in web development, Linux systems, and technical communication. With a career built around simplifying complex ideas, I combine technical expertise with a talent for clear, engaging instruction. My work reflects both hands-on experience in the industry and a dedication to helping others navigate today's fast-changing digital world.

For eleven years, I taught at Apple, where I guided learners of all backgrounds in understanding and mastering their devices. That role sharpened my ability to break down advanced concepts into accessible steps, laying the groundwork for my transition into technical writing and authorship. My approach emphasizes clarity, accuracy, and real-world application, making me an effective communicator across a range of technologies.

Now a self-published technology author, I create books and articles that serve as practical resources for readers seeking confidence in areas like Linux, web development, and more with a focus on new learners. By blending technical depth with clear instruction, I continue to build a portfolio that reflects both a professional experience and my commitment to empowering others through technology.
