I am a technology professional and author with a strong foundation in web development, Linux systems, and technical communication. With a career built around simplifying complex ideas, I combine technical expertise with a talent for clear, engaging instruction. My work reflects both hands-on experience in the industry and a dedication to helping others navigate today's fast-changing digital world.

For eleven years, I taught at Apple, where I guided learners of all backgrounds in understanding and mastering their devices. That role sharpened my ability to break down advanced concepts into accessible steps, laying the groundwork for my transition into technical writing and authorship.

I enjoyed my time at Apple, but after experiencing two strokes, I found myself no longer able to keep up with the fast pace. The strokes I've experienced affected my balance, which makes walking and driving very difficult. I've been spending my time focused on healing and rehabilitation. But now, I want to concentrate on teaching technology through my writing.
